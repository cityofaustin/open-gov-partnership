Introducing Austin’s Flagship Open Government Campaign

In April 2016, Austin was selected as the only U.S. local government partner for the Open Government Partnership (OGP). The OGP is a unique international coalition of governments committed to the ideals of transparency, accountability and participation. As part of the partnership, over the next 3 years Austinites will work together to revitalize the public square. Sign up for our email list to learn more as the campaign moves forward.

Open & Honest

Austin partnered in crafting groundbreaking legislation that allowed elected officials, for the first time, to communicate with each other between posted meetings on an electronic bulletin board. To open the process to crystal transparency, the City built the first readable-in-real-time, searchable online message board for citizens to know what their representatives were discussing 24/7/365.
